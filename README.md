# 🚀 DenPartner

DenPartner is an affiliate marketing platform that helps users earn money online by sharing campaign links.

---

## 💡 What is DenPartner?

DenPartner allows anyone to:
- Share product links
- Promote offers
- Earn commission on every successful order

No investment required. Work from home.

---

## 💰 How You Earn

1. Select a campaign
2. Click "Get Link"
3. Share it on WhatsApp / social media
4. When someone buys → you earn commission

---

## 💼 Payments

- Earnings are credited to your wallet after verification
- Minimum withdrawal: ₹10
- Withdraw anytime
- Amount is transferred within 24 hours
